## Eval Checklist: LinkedIn Draft

1. HOOK: Does the first line (before any line break) create curiosity or state a surprising fact — WITHOUT starting with "I" or a question?
   → Yes/No

2. VOICE: Does it sound like a specific person sharing a real opinion (not a generic "thought leader" post)? Does it have a clear point of view and conviction?
   → Yes/No

3. BANNED_PHRASES: Is it free of ALL these phrases: "here's the kicker", "the numbers speak volumes", "let that sink in", "but here's the thing", "the writing is on the wall", "buckle up", "game-changer", "paradigm shift", "in an era where", "let's look at", "now let's digest", "the broader implication", "the challenge is clear"?
   → Yes/No

4. STRUCTURE: Does it use short paragraphs (≤3 lines each) with at least one line break between every paragraph? No wall-of-text blocks?
   → Yes/No

5. SUBSTANCE: Does it include at least one specific data point, named company/person, or concrete example (not vague generalizations)?
   → Yes/No

6. ANTI_GAMING: Read as a LinkedIn user scrolling their feed. Would you genuinely stop and read this? Not technically — genuinely.
   → Yes/No
